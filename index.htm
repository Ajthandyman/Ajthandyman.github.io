Replacing broken fence posts. Making good after other trades. Helping you complete or fix a DIY job that you’ve started. Giving advice. I don’t charge for every little job, just my time - so if I swap a light fitting, repair a broken curtain rail bracket and adjust a door to fit then it’s just the time (+ parts) cost.  Small jobs only please. I don’t quote for jobs - I will tell you the approx material costs and an estimate of time. A job takes as long as it takes. If you’re unhappy with my speed, settle up and send me on my way.
